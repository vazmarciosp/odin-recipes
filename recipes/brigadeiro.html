Brigadeiro

Home

Description

Brigadeiro is a classic Brazilian chocolate truffle made from condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter, slowly cooked until thick and then rolled into bite-sized balls.

Traditionally covered in chocolate sprinkles, these sweet treats are a must-have at birthday parties and celebrations and are loved by both children and adults.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (395 g) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Chocolate sprinkles for rolling

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter.
  2. Cook over medium-low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and starts to pull away from the bottom of the pan.
  3. Remove from the heat, stir in the vanilla extract if using, and let the mixture cool completely on a greased plate or dish.
  4. Once cool, grease your hands lightly with butter, scoop small portions of the mixture, and roll them into bite-sized balls.
  5. Roll each ball in chocolate sprinkles until fully coated and place them in paper candy cups or on a serving plate.
